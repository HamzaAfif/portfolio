My Story & Vision

"Imagination is more important than knowledge." — Albert Einstein

"You never stopped surprising me, since I met you, with your talents. Never stop believing in yourself and your abilities."
— Mohamed Salihi, Teacher

Returning to Morocco after my time in Ukraine wasn't just a change of place—it was a turning point in my life. I had been pursuing a degree in pharmacy when circumstances beyond my control forced me to leave everything behind and start over. That experience shaped me in ways I didn't expect, teaching me resilience and fueling my determination to channel my passion for technology into something meaningful.

I knew I didn't see the world the same way as others during a moment in class. A teacher asked, "If you had 30 million euros, would you stop working and learning?" While everyone else eagerly said yes, I couldn't help but think differently. To me, that kind of opportunity wouldn't mean stopping—it would mean doing more, learning more, and exploring even further.

My Vision

I envision a future where technology serves as a bridge—connecting communities, solving complex problems, and creating opportunities for growth. My goal is to continue building tools and platforms that are accessible, innovative, and impactful, whether for small businesses, educational institutions, or underserved communities.
