Digital Admission Portal

A PHP and MySQL-based solution developed for my school to digitalize the admission process for students, teachers, and administrative staff. This portal simplifies and streamlines admissions, document approvals, exam scheduling, grading, and results tracking across various academic programs and levels.

Explore Features

Project Overview

The Digital Admission Portal was built to replace manual workflows with an efficient digital system. Below are its key functionalities:

  • Students:
    • Log in to their personal accounts.
    • Upload required admission documents.
    • Track the progress of their application (percentage completion).
    • Choose a preferred date and time for their exam session.
    • Receive their exam convocation directly via email (PDF).
  • Teachers:
    • Log in to their dashboard to manage exams.
    • Upload written exams and manage oral assessments.
    • Grade studentsâ€™ performance for written and oral exams.
    • Collaborate with admission officers for special exam sessions.
  • Admission Officers:
    • Review student documents and approve or deny them with feedback.
    • Manage exam sessions by creating, editing, and assigning sessions.
    • Create and manage teacher accounts.
    • Track assigned students by filtering data (e.g., name, ID, year, major).
  • Admin Dashboard:
    • Manage users (students, teachers, admission officers).
    • Search and filter students by year, major, status, or name.
    • Request exams from teachers for specific sessions or programs.
  • Multilevel Support: Handles all academic years (1st to 4th) and multiple programs (e.g., business and engineering).

Key Features

  • Document Management: Students upload required documents, and admission officers approve or deny them with detailed feedback.
  • Exam Scheduling: Students choose their preferred date and time for exam sessions.
  • Email Integration: Convocation emails with PDF attachments are sent directly to students after session selection.
  • Teacher Dashboard: Teachers upload exams, manage oral assessments, and assign grades.
  • Administrative Tools: Admins manage users and filter data with advanced search capabilities.
  • Real-Time Progress Tracking: Students track the status of their application in percentages (e.g., 50%, 80%, 100%).
  • Oral Exam Management: Teachers conduct and assess oral exams directly through the portal.
